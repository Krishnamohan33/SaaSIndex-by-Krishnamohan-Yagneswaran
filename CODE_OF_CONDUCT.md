# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SaaSIndex is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone who contributes to or interacts with this project.

We welcome contributors from all backgrounds and experience levels.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in communication
- Provide constructive and relevant feedback
- Respect differing viewpoints
- Act in good faith toward the project and community
- Help maintain a positive and collaborative environment

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or inflammatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from participation.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it privately through the contact information provided on the SaaSIndex website or repository.

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any official communication channels related to SaaSIndex

---

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by common open-source community standards and is intended to promote a healthy, professional collaboration environment.
