## What You Can Contribute

You may contribute by:

- Adding new B2B SaaS products
- Improving product descriptions
- Updating categories or pricing models
- Fixing incorrect or outdated information
- Improving documentation or structure

All contributions should be factual, neutral, and based on publicly available information.

---

## Contribution Rules

To maintain quality and safety:

- All contributions must be submitted via **Pull Request**
- No direct commits to the main branch
- Product data must follow the existing **JSON schema**
- Content must be **plain text only**
  - ‚ùå No HTML
  - ‚ùå No scripts
  - ‚ùå No marketing or promotional language
- Do not add affiliate links or tracking parameters

Submissions that do not meet these standards may be rejected.

---

## Product Data Guidelines

When adding or editing products:

- The product must have an **official public website**
- Use clear, descriptive language
- Avoid subjective opinions or hype
- Keep pros and cons factual and concise
- Reference only publicly available sources

---

## Review Process

1. Submit a pull request
2. Maintainers review for accuracy, clarity, and structure
3. Changes may be requested
4. Approved contributions are merged

---

## Credit Policy

- Approved contributors are **credited publicly on the SaaSIndex website**
- Credits may include name, username, or profile link
- Contributions do **not** imply ownership, employment, or revenue sharing

---

## Sponsored Products

Sponsored listings are managed separately and **cannot** be added through pull requests.

If you are interested in sponsored listings, please use the official sponsorship form on the SaaSIndex website.
